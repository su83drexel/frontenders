FrontEnders Team: Adriana Vasquez, Lize Simpson, Shehryar Usman, Ian Lahet, and Chase Padilla

About Us

Our project is a web app similar to websites like Letterboxd and IMDb, except we will be focusing on and recommending bad movies instead. We are planning to create this as a social website where users can find, review, and leave comments over movies. We will allow the users to be able to search for movies where they can choose how to rank/filter them dynamically.
